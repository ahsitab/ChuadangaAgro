About Chuadanga Agro

Our story and mission

Connecting Farmers to Consumers

Founded in 1997, Chuadanga Agro has been a trusted name in agricultural products in the region. We specialize in providing high-quality livestock, fruits, and crops to customers and businesses alike.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between local farmers and consumers, ensuring fair prices for producers and premium quality for buyers.
